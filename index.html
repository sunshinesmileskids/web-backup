About

Hello my name is Henny!

Taking care of children is my passion. I love to spend time creating lasting bonds with my clients. Watching a child grow and helping them develop is one of the most rewarding experiences I have had. I have over 10 years of experience as a nanny, babysitter, and child caregiver in Bali and the surrounding area. I am fully CPR certified.

My approach to child care is to provide the same level of love and care as I would for my own children. I understand that feeling confident in your child's caregiver is extremely important in allowing you to relax and enjoy your time. Lets get to know each other so I can help!
